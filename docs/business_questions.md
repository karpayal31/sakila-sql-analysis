## Customer & Rental Behaviour Analysis — Business Questions

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The goal of this analysis is to understand **customer behaviour, revenue patterns, and movie performance** using the DVD rental database.
The insights generated will help businesses improve **customer retention, marketing strategy, and content decisions**.

---

## 📊 Analysis Goals

This project aims to answer key business questions across four main areas:

1. Customer Behaviour
2. Revenue & Sales Performance
3. Movie & Category Performance
4. Operational Insights

---

## 👥 1. Customer Behaviour Analysis

**Goal:** Understand how customers interact with the business and identify valuable customers.

### Business Questions

* Who are the top customers based on total spending?
* Which customers rent most frequently?
* Which customers are inactive or at risk of churn?
* What is the lifetime value of each customer?
* How can customers be segmented into High, Medium, and Low value groups?
* What categories or genres does each customer prefer?

### Business Value

* Targeted marketing campaigns
* Customer retention strategies
* Personalized recommendations

---

## 💰 2. Revenue & Sales Performance

**Goal:** Identify how revenue is generated and track financial performance trends.

### Business Questions

* What is the total revenue generated over time?
* Which months or periods generate the highest revenue?
* What is the running (cumulative) revenue trend?
* Which customers contribute most to total revenue?
* How evenly is revenue distributed among customers?

### Business Value

* Forecast revenue trends
* Optimize pricing and promotions
* Identify revenue concentration risks

---

## 🎬 3. Movie & Category Performance

**Goal:** Determine which content drives engagement and revenue.

### Business Questions

* Which movies generate the highest revenue?
* What is the top-performing movie within each category?
* Which categories are most popular among customers?
* Are some categories underperforming?
* Which films are rented frequently but generate low revenue?

### Business Value

* Improve content acquisition decisions
* Optimize catalog offerings
* Enhance recommendation systems

---

## ⚙️ 4. Operational Insights

**Goal:** Understand rental patterns and operational efficiency.

### Business Questions

* What are peak rental days or times?
* How often is inventory utilized?
* Which films experience high rental demand?
* Are there seasonal rental patterns?
* How does rental activity vary across locations or stores?

### Business Value

* Inventory optimization
* Staffing and operational planning
* Demand forecasting

---

## 📈 Expected Outcomes

This analysis will produce:

* Customer segmentation insights
* Revenue performance dashboards
* Category and movie rankings
* Behavioural trends over time
* Data-driven business recommendations

---

## 🧠 Analytical Approach

The analysis will involve:

* SQL joins across related tables
* Aggregations and window functions
* Ranking and cumulative calculations
* Customer segmentation techniques (RFM analysis)
* Trend and performance evaluation

---

## ✅ Success Criteria

The project is successful if it:

* Identifies high-value customers
* Explains revenue drivers clearly
* Highlights top-performing content
* Provides actionable business insights
* Demonstrates clear understanding of database relationships
